⭒☆⭒☆━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━☆⭒☆⭒

  • - Ingredients ✿ :
  • Pancake flour
  • Melted butter/margarine
  • 1 Egg
  • Milk
  • Strawberries
  • Chocolate ice cream (optional)
  • - Tools ✿ :
  • Pan
  • Measure glass (or bowl if you dont have one)
  • Spatula
  • Spoon
  • Plate (recommended using glass plate for prettier looks ヾ๑ˊᵕˋ๑◞♡ )
  • - Steps ✿ :
  • - First, put all of the pancake flour to the bowl
  • - Second, pour the melted margarine into the bowl
  • - Then, crack the egg and pour to the egg
  • - After that, add milk (highly recommended to put more milk for smoother dough ヾ๑ˊᵕˋ๑◞♡)
  • - Stir it well until the dough is really smooth
  • - And let the dough for 10 minutes
  • - After let the dough for 10 mins, pour the dough to the pan (dont forget to put butter before it!)
  • - Then, let the bottom side of dough, after its already 2 mins, flip the pancake
  • notes: we cannot let the pancake dough too long because its easily overcooked
  • - Put the pancake to the plate
  • - Add some garnish, it depends on your creativity
  • - Put one scoop of ice cream on top of the pancake
  • - Put strawberries on the ice cream, and tadaaaa
  • Your pancake is ready to be served (ꈍ ꒳ ꈍ✿)
